Department of Electrical & Electronic Engineering

Welcome to the EEE department at East West University. B.Sc in EEE is accredited by the Board of Accreditation for Engineering and Technical Education (BAETE), an independent body formed under the umbrella of the Institute of Engineers, Bangladesh (IEB). BAETE is the premier authority in Bangladesh for accreditation of all engineering programs. This accreditation is a testimony of the high quality of education imparted to the students of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at East West University.

EEE department started its journey in January 2005. Within the last few years, we have been able to establish ourselves as a leading EEE department in the country.

The profession of electrical and electronic engineers has truly become global during the recent years. The unprecedented mobility of knowledge, technology, information and capital has redefined electrical and electronic engineering. We have taken up the challenge of producing quality graduates who can become leaders in the global arena.

I invite bright young students with the ambition to become industry leaders to join our program. We are always looking for outstanding faculty members in our team and you are welcome to let us know about you.

B.Sc in EEE program has been accreditation by Board of Accreditation for Engineering and Technical Education (BAETE), IEB. Our Graduates are eligible for applying membership of Institution of Engineers, Bangladesh

Please explore our website and know more about us. I look forward to hearing your feedback.
